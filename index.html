Attack on Titan

About Attack on Titan

Attack on Titan, AOT for short, is a Japanese anime series set in a world where humanity is endangered by enormous humanistic creatures known as Titans. The story primarily follows three characters: Eren Yeager, Mikasa Ackerman, and Armen Arlet. They join the military to fight against the Titans after their hometown is destroyed by them.

Why Attack on Titan is my favorite show

  • For an anime show, it is incredibly deep and thought out.
  • I really like the animation style.
  • It shows a war in which you can understand the point of view from both sides. You can even understand Eren's point of view when he goes from hero to villain, even if you may not agree with him, and I think that's incredibly important.
  • There are so many layers to the story.
